What is an E-Commerce Website?

An e-commerce website is a platform that allows businesses and individuals to buy and sell goods and services over the internet. These websites are convenient, efficient, and offer access to a vast array of products from the comfort of your home or mobile device.

Our e-commerce website, ColorCart, was developed to demonstrate how modern online stores function. It features a visually engaging homepage, product listings, a customer form for placing orders, a shopping cart that updates dynamically, and essential informational sections like 'About Us' and 'Privacy Policy.'

This project is ideal for learning and showcasing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript skills in a practical and creative way. It's a reflection of how user interaction, design, and backend logic come together in real-world web development.
